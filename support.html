Support

SubKiller is a private, local-only iOS app. Have a question, found a service we don't support, or hitting a bug? Email is the fastest way to reach us.

Email: jorgelameiras207@gmail.com

We try to reply within 48 hours. Include your iOS version and what you were doing when the issue happened — it speeds things up a lot.

Common questions

My subscription service isn't in the cancel-link library.

Email us the service name and we'll add it. The library ships v1 with 100 services and grows from user reports. In the meantime, every subscription you add will still surface in your renewal reminders and lifetime-paid totals.

Does SubKiller see my bank account?

No. There is no Plaid login, no transaction reading, no bank API. We don't ask for your bank credentials and we don't have a backend that could store them. Your subscription list lives on your phone.

I bought Pro but it didn't unlock.

Open Settings → Restore purchase in SubKiller. If that doesn't restore it, email us with the date of purchase and the Apple ID you used. We can verify with RevenueCat and fix it from our side.

Does it auto-cancel for me?

No, intentionally. Auto-cancel would require storing your login credentials for every service — exactly the kind of attack surface SubKiller exists to avoid. Instead, SubKiller opens the exact cancel URL in Safari, tells you the retention sequence the vendor will use, and verifies 24 hours later that it actually worked.

What about Android?

Not in v1. We're focused on shipping a great iOS app first. If demand is there, Android is on the roadmap.

Privacy

Anonymous analytics are off by default. There is no account, no cloud sync, no email scan, no bank login. See our privacy pledge for the technical details — what data does and does not leave your phone.
